🦍 Monkey Climb — Stage 1
Real Gorilla Tag locomotion test. In VR: squeeze the grip button to grab any surface — trees, walls, bars, or even the ground itself — then move your hand to pull your whole body around. Let go to fling yourself with real momentum. There's no swinging in open air — just like the real game, you always need to actually touch something to move. Crouch down and grab the floor to push yourself along the ground. On desktop: WASD + mouse to fly around and preview the map.
