🦍 Monkey Climb — Stage 1
Real Gorilla Tag locomotion test. In VR: just swing your hand onto any surface — a tree, wall, or bar — no button, no need to crouch. Even a quick swing will register now, not just a slow deliberate hold. Move your hand while touching to pull your whole body around, and let go to fling yourself with real momentum. Hands glow when they're touching something. On desktop: WASD + mouse to fly around and preview the map.
